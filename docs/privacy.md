# ZedMark Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 30, 2026

ZedMark is a local Markdown reader for macOS.

ZedMark does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data. Documents opened in ZedMark remain on your Mac unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

The app may store local preferences on your device, such as selected theme, language, and recent-file access bookmarks, so the app can reopen files you previously selected. This information is stored locally on your Mac and is not sent to the developer.

ZedMark does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue on GitHub:

[https://github.com/ieaglez/ZedMark/issues](https://github.com/ieaglez/ZedMark/issues)
